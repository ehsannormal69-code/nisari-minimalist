01 — About

A home built
on love & togetherness

Nisari is more than a house — it is the gathering place for a family rooted in the rich coastal soil of Vadakara, Kerala. Here, laughter echoes through the halls, children's footsteps patter across the floors, and generations come together as one.

Founded by Abdul Nasser and Subaida, this home has blossomed into a vibrant tapestry of children, grandchildren, and boundless love. Every corner holds a memory; every room tells a story.

In the heart of Vadakara, amidst coconut groves and warm breezes, Nisari stands as a testament to family, faith, and the beauty of shared lives.

03 — Roots

Vadakara —
where it all began

Nestled in the Kozhikode district of Kerala, Vadakara is a coastal town known for its serene backwaters, lush coconut groves, and the timeless rhythm of the Arabian Sea. It is here that Nisari finds its foundation.

The house sits amidst the gentle landscape of northern Kerala, where the air carries the scent of spices and the sound of temple bells blends with the call to prayer. It is a place where tradition and tranquility live side by side.
